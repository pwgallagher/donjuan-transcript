THE CANONICAL location of this edition of Don Juan is:

https://petergallagher.net/byrons-don-juan-cantos-1-5-annotated

THIS edition —like my earlier “annotated+narrated” version of Cantos I & II (2019)—has also been uploaded to Archive.org in an attempt to preserve its availability; if not for another two centuries (does anything last that long any more?), at least for many years. On the date of publication, the URL was:

https://archive.org/details/@pwgallagher

This edition can also be found on Zenodo.org where it has the DOI:

10.5281/zenodo.19495353

Peter Gallagher (@pwgallagher on X.com) is a student of classics and photography. His older recordings of several Cantos of Don Juan (V, XII-XVI) are available at librivox.org. He uploads small PDF books of his photography and other documents from time to time to petergallagher.net, where you will also find the latest version of this document
